Cash management logistics

Processed payments of more than 22,000 inbound passengers on arrival for a repeat Covid-19 Day 2 and 7 test in Nigeria. This was achieved through partnerships with the Federal Government, State Government, Sterling Bank, and Private labs across all the International Airports and States in the Country in which operations started in September 2020. Provided an efficient Data Management system analyzed to influence economic strategic decisions pertaining to the Covid-19 response. Our services included:

  • Registration of Inbound passengers on the Nigerian Travel Portal
  • Collection and processing of payments received from passengers
  • Data Management system
  • Collaborate with private labs in ensuring tests are conducted for the passengers
  • Real-Time response to payments-related issues for both online and on-arrival payments
